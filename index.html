About RAPHA Therapies

RAPHA Therapies was founded with a vision to provide comprehensive, holistic support services that address the unique needs of each individual. The name "RAPHA" reflects our commitment to healing, restoration, and growth.

Our interdisciplinary team brings together diverse expertise in life coaching, occupational therapy, education, social work, and emotional development to offer truly integrated care. We believe in treating the whole person, not just isolated symptoms or challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and approach.

We offer complimentary initial consultations to help determine which services would best meet your needs. During this session, we'll discuss your concerns, goals, and circumstances to develop a personalized recommendation. Our interdisciplinary approach allows us to provide comprehensive support or connect you with the most appropriate specialist.

The process begins with an initial consultation, followed by an assessment to understand your specific needs. We then develop a personalized plan and schedule regular sessions based on your goals and availability. Our team provides ongoing support and adjusts the approach as needed to ensure you're making progress toward your objectives.

Insurance coverage varies depending on the service. Some of our therapeutic services may be covered by certain insurance plans, while others are typically self-pay. During your initial consultation, we'll discuss payment options and provide information about potential insurance coverage, sliding scale fees, and other financial arrangements to make our services accessible.

We offer both in-person and virtual options for many of our services. Our telehealth platform allows us to provide secure, effective support regardless of your location. Some specialized services may require in-person sessions, while others can be fully delivered through virtual means. We'll discuss the best format for your specific needs during your consultation.
